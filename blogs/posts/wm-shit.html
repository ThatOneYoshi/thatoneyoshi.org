WiiMart? What is it?

WiiMart is a revival of the Wii Shop Channel, giving back the nostalgia of when you were a kid and you got a Wii Points card and wanted a new game to play, or if your parent gave you a choice of an WiiWare or virtual console game to get.

and WiiMart was made by Ocarina, which he began working on it after the unsuccess of Rii Shop.
